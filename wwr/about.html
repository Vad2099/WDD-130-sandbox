Amazing Adventure Rafting Club

Our mission:

Inspiring and Facilitating Unforgettable Adventures in the Heart of Nature. We are on a continual quest for excellence in the river and mountain adventure industry. Our mission is to enrich people's lives through unique and thrilling experiences in nature, all while promoting environmental responsibility and respect for our natural surroundings.

History

"In the picturesque town of Valle de Bravo, nestled in the heart of the State of Mexico, our journey began. It was the year 1990 when a group of nature enthusiasts and extreme sports lovers came together with a shared vision. Inspired by the breathtaking landscapes and the thrill of the great outdoors, they decided to form our club. From the very beginning, our mission has been to provide unforgettable adventures in the midst of nature's wonders. Over the years, we've grown and evolved, but our passion for outdoor exploration and our commitment to safety and environmental stewardship have remained unwavering. Today, we are proud to continue our legacy as a leading provider of river and mountain adventures, offering our clients the opportunity to experience the beauty and excitement that Valle de Bravo and its surrounding areas have to offer. Our story is one of adventure, camaraderie, and a deep appreciation for the natural world, and we look forward to sharing it with you."
