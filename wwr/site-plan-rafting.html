Overview

Purpose

the purpose of establishing an online presence for the rafting business is to reach a broader audience, increase bookings, build trust, and provide valuable information while fostering customer engagement and loyalty. It's a strategic move to grow the business and stay competitive in the adventure tourism industry.

Audience

The target audience for the water rafting website is adventure-seeking individuals and groups who are interested in experiencing the thrill of white-water rafting and outdoor enthusiasts looking for memorable and adrenaline-pumping activities.
